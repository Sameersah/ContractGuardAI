EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on January 1, 2025, between TechCorp Inc. ("Company") and Employee ("Employee").

TERM OF EMPLOYMENT:
The term of this Agreement shall be 24 months, commencing on January 15, 2025.

NON-COMPETE CLAUSE:
Employee agrees not to compete with Company for a period of 12 months following termination, in any industry, anywhere in the United States. This restriction applies to all business activities and geographic locations.

INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY:
All work, inventions, and intellectual property created by Employee during the term of employment, including work done on personal time and personal projects, shall be the exclusive property of Company. Employee hereby assigns all rights, title, and interest in such work to Company.

TERMINATION:
Employee must provide 90 days written notice prior to termination. Company may terminate this Agreement at any time with 30 days notice. Employee agrees to complete all pending work during the notice period.

SEVERANCE:
Upon termination by Company without cause, Employee shall receive severance equal to 1 week of salary per year of service, with a minimum of 1 week.

PAID TIME OFF:
Employee shall receive 10 days of paid time off per year, which may not be carried over to the following year.

SALARY:
Employee shall receive an annual salary of $120,000, payable in bi-weekly installments.

WORK LOCATION:
Employee shall work from the Company's headquarters located at 123 Tech Street, San Francisco, CA. Remote work is not permitted without prior written approval.

This Agreement expires on December 31, 2025, unless renewed by mutual written consent of both parties.
